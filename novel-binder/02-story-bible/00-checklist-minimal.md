## Minimal Story Bible Checklist

1. Premise
2. Genre and audience
3. Theme
4. Main characters
5. Setting/world rules
6. Plot beats
7. Scene list
8. Timeline
9. Continuity notes

## Best practice

Don’t try to fill all of this at once. Most authors build a story bible in layers: concept first, then characters and structure, then worldbuilding and continuity, then revision canon.
